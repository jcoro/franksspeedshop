About Frank's Speed Shop

Franks Speed Shop was established in 1974. With over 30 years experience in the automotive industry, rest assured that whether you are in need of a tune up or a custom built hot rod, we are here for you.

Franks Speed Shop specializes in both late & early model GM, Ford as well as Mopar vehicles. Be sure to visit our services page for a overview of the services we offer.

There is no job to small or too large for us. You will not be disappointed with our fast, friendly, and best of all affordable service.

Thank you for choosing Franks Speed Shop.
